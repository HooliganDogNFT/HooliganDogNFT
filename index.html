Hooligan Dogs Community

The Hooligan Dog Community is an NFT collection of 10,000 unique ERC-721 tokens stored on the polygon Blockchain. Each will be computer generated from 300 + hand drawn traits consisting of various rarities and will be non-sequentially minted at one price for fair distribution. Each NFT comes with exclusive rights and a membership to the Hooligan Dog Community, including functioning utilities and ever-growing club features. The possibilities are limitless, just like the everlasting life spans of these other worldly Nudies on the blockchain.

Check out our roadmap and utility for exciting plans for the future!

Hooligan Dogs Co llection

Many years ago 10,000 dogs were sent to earth to evolve the human population.

These dogs were the faithful companions of historical and mythological characters, but also contemporary ones who existed on earth. The dog community acquired human characteristics and became the reincarnation of its owners. The world after the arrival of these dogs was no longer the same and there was a division in circles:
Hell, in the shape of an inverted cone, is a dark funnel at the bottom of which the angel of Evil, the rebel Lucifer, is stuck, thus placed in the farthest point of the entire universe.
The underground world is a representation of all earthly evils and represents the diabolical and evil part of society here are the protagonists of the struggles that have torn the whole world apart. After the descent into the underworld, we ascend to the northern hemisphere, where the Mountain of the living rises; here are those who are about to seize the moment when they can ascend before the Creator and place themselves among the blessed. The journey continues and you arrive in Heaven, here there are celestial figures ready to take flight and endowed with divine aura. Once you have crossed all the realms and thus possessing all the necessary characteristics, you can reach the dimension of the Ultraverse, a dimension superior to all where time and space are no longer as we imagine them, and where figures with great powers reign.
